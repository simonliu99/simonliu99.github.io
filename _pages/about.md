I am a senior undergraduate student at the [Johns Hopkins University](https://www.jhu.edu/) majoring in biomedical engineering and computer science. My research interests include applications of machine learning in predicting patient outcomes and medical device development. I currently work in the [Neuroplastic Surgery Research Laboratory](https://www.snps2018.org/neuroplastic-surgery-lab), led by Professor Chad Gordon, as well as with the [Wu Lab](https://www.medschool.umaryland.edu/anesthesiology/research/junfang-wu-bm-phd/team/) in the STAR-ORC at the University of Maryland School of Medicine.

I am expecting to receive a B.S. in Biomedical Engineering in May 2021 from the Johns Hopkins University. I will then attend the postbaccalaureate intramural program at the NIH in the [Medical Genomics Unit](https://www.genome.gov/about-nhgri/Division-of-Intramural-Research/Medical-Genetics-Branch) under Dr. Ben Solomon. Ultimately, I plan to pursue an Md/PhD program and become a physician-scientist.

In my spare time, I enjoy tinkering with servers and electronics as well as hiking and other outdoor activities.
